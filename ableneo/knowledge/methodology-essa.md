# ESSA Methodology

Ableneo's proprietary method for AI adoption in regulated and legacy environments. It is
the named framework behind delivery, and it appears in campaign copy, nurture sequences,
and the gated "Lídri transformácie" report as the Ableneo point of view.

## Why it exists
AI pilots fail in banks and insurers not for technical reasons but for organizational and
governance reasons: unclear business result, no production path, no accountability. ESSA
is the discipline that carries a use case from idea to a governed system in production.

## How to use it in content
- Frame ESSA as method, not jargon. Show the steps through a real project arc.
- Tie it to the anti-pilot proof point (most projects reach production).
- It pairs with the governance layer of the offering: `offering.md`.

Pillars (verify wording): governance/accountability, data/clarity, people/meaning, and
the production discipline that ties them together. Cross-check `brand-core.md` belief
