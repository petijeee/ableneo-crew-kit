# Logo & Iconography

> Source of truth: **ABLENEO Design Guidelines 2025**.

## The logo
Two core elements that form one signature:
- **Logotype**, the lowercase wordmark `ableneo`.
- **Logo mark**, the network-node symbol (connected circles), expressing aligned, linked intelligence.
- **Tagline**, *Intelligence that works* (locked under the wordmark in the full lockup).

### Approved color variants
1. **On dark**, Lime mark + white wordmark on Charcoal.
2. **On coloured (Lime)**, white mark + Charcoal wordmark on Lime.
3. **On light**, Lime mark + Charcoal wordmark on White.
4. **Monochrome**, official **black** or **white** only, chosen for contrast, when color is unavailable.

### Construction & minimum size
- **33 mm** minimum width for the **full logo (with tagline)**.
- **20 mm** minimum width for the logo **without tagline**.
- Built on a consistent grid for balance across formats.

### Safe zone
- Minimum clear space = the **diameter of the largest circle** in the logo mark, on all sides.
- No graphic element, text, or imagery may intrude into the safe zone. Applies to every variation and application.

### Misuse: never do this
- ❌ Alter the typeface / logotype style
- ❌ Change the color (outside approved variants)
- ❌ Rotate the logo
- ❌ Distort or change proportions
- ❌ Apply shading, gradients, or other effects
- ❌ Use the logo in outline form
- ❌ Place on low-contrast / busy backgrounds

> When unsure, use an approved variant at correct proportion with full safe zone. Don't improvise.

## Iconography
Two complementary styles, one geometric system (rounded edges, consistent stroke weight):

| Style | Use for |
|---|---|
| **Solid** | Highlights and elements needing stronger visual emphasis. |
| **Linear** | Supportive or secondary functions where a lighter appearance fits. |

### Icon color
- Maintain sufficient contrast on every background (light and dark).
- Approved: Lime-fill, Charcoal-fill, white/charcoal line on appropriate backgrounds, Steel-grey fills.
- ❌ Avoid low-contrast or inconsistent combinations (e.g. pale icon on pale ground), it kills legibility and weakens impact.

> Same principle as the color system: clarity and contrast first, one chromatic family at a time.
